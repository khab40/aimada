Market abuse detection, simulated live.

Markets move in milliseconds. But manipulation hides inside normal-looking order flow.

The Arena turns that invisible behaviour into a live red-team versus blue-team simulation.

First, the market establishes a baseline: liquidity, orders, trades, and normal agent behaviour.

Then the red team enters. An abusive strategy creates pressure in the order book and tests the defence.

The blue team reacts with detection, risk scoring, and incident evidence that executives can understand.

Scenarios can be generated, replayed, and compared in a safe market lab before anything reaches production.

Tournament mode extends the Arena into multi-user simulations: teams can attack, defend, score, and learn together.

This creates a practical training ground for surveillance teams, model evaluators, product leaders, and regulators.

The About page shows the research foundation behind the project: agent-based market simulation, limit order books, spoofing, realism metrics, and financial-market experiments.

For leadership, the value is simple: clearer risk visibility, faster experimentation, and a stronger compliance narrative.
