# Safety and Disclaimers

AI Market Abuse Detection Arena is a synthetic simulation and detector evaluation project.

## Not Production Surveillance

The project does not provide legal, compliance, trading, or market surveillance advice. It should not be used to make production enforcement or trading decisions.

## Synthetic Behavior

Scenario agents are designed to produce abuse-like patterns in a controlled environment. They are not instructions for real market activity, and outputs should be interpreted only in the context of synthetic data.

## AI-Generated Explanations

AI explanations and reports are summaries of simulated events and detector outputs. They may be incomplete or wrong and should be reviewed against the underlying event log.

## Data Handling

Do not add private trading data, credentials, or confidential records to the repository. Use `.env` for local secrets and keep generated outputs out of source control unless they are intentionally curated samples.
